Cities I Have Lived In

  • Seoul ♥ A vibrant and well-connected city.

    Seoul is a large city with excellent transportation, modern infrastructure, universities, cultural centers, parks, malls, diverse restaurants, and many churches within a lively environment.

  • Granada ★ Rich culture and seasonal celebrations.

    Granada is a cool city known for shopping malls, beautiful fashion, cultural festivals, Christmas and Easter celebrations, and an active lifestyle even in hot weather.

  • West Haven ★ A calm and welcoming home.

    West Haven is my current city, and I love its peaceful environment, friendly people, and overall happiness.

Top 3 Cities I Would Like to Visit

  1. Toronto

    Toronto is a major tech hub with strong AI research, networking opportunities, and one of the most multicultural data-driven economies.

  2. Seattle, WA

    Seattle is a technology center with companies like Amazon and Microsoft, known for cloud computing and high-impact innovation.

  3. Geneva

    Geneva offers beautiful lake views, a calm international atmosphere, and a blend of nature, culture, and global institutions.
